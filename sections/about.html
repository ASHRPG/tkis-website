Our Journey

Shaping Future Leaders Since 2011

The Kolkata International School (TKIS) is one of the best CBSE schools in Liluah, Howrah with premium lush green campus and high quality education.

Run by the S.N.R. Memorial Trust, our school follows the Central Board of Secondary Education (CBSE) curriculum. The journey began in 2011 by introducing our first CBSE School in Howrah. With quality-driven systemic processes and a dedicated team of teaching professionals, we foster young talent to become successful leaders of tomorrow.
